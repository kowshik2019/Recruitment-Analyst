Job Title: Senior Data Engineer
Company: FinTech Innovations Inc.
Location: Remote (US)
Employment Type: Full-Time

ABOUT THE ROLE
We are looking for a Senior Data Engineer to join our growing Data Platform team.
You will design, build, and maintain our cloud-native data infrastructure,
support ML engineering teams, and lead data quality initiatives.

REQUIRED SKILLS & QUALIFICATIONS
- 5+ years of experience in data engineering
- Strong proficiency in Python and SQL
- Apache Spark / PySpark (must-have)
- Apache Kafka or similar streaming technology
- Snowflake or equivalent cloud data warehouse
- Apache Airflow for pipeline orchestration
- dbt for data transformation
- AWS or GCP cloud experience
- Experience with ML pipeline support (MLflow, feature stores, model serving)
- Docker and containerisation
- Strong understanding of data modelling (Star Schema, Data Vault)
- Data quality frameworks (Great Expectations or similar)

NICE TO HAVE
- Terraform or infrastructure-as-code experience
- LangChain or LLM pipeline experience
- Databricks certification
- Real-time streaming at scale (>1M events/day)
- Financial services or fintech domain experience

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Design and build scalable, reliable data pipelines
- Maintain and optimise our Snowflake data warehouse
- Support ML engineers with data infrastructure and feature engineering
- Implement data quality and observability frameworks
- Collaborate with data scientists, analysts, and product teams
- Lead data architecture reviews and documentation

COMPENSATION
$140,000 – $175,000 annually + equity + benefits
